Experiences

As a Business Techonology Analyst at Deloitte Consulting LLP, I specialize as a Front-End Software Engineer on the SEMOSS platform, a web-based, end-to-end data analytics and visualization tool. This application allows users to effortlessly connect data from various different data sources, run analytic algorithms, transform data to cavas-based visualizations to discover insights, and then publish to share the insight with anyone.

As the Creator and Lead Engineer of Smart Vibe, I designed and developed a smart shock absorber for the tennis racket that can provide insight for the player about their performance. After recieving funding from Johns Hopkins University Engineering Department to pursue this idea, I spent a summer at the University of California, Berkeley where I embedded a rechargeable sensor (9-axis IMU). This process involved 3D Printing, computer-aided design in PTC Creo, injection molding, and data anlytics using Matlab. Click on the image to learn more about this project!

As a R&D Scientist at Genscape, I independently designed and implemented an automated program written in Python that uniquely identifies and counts vehicle traffic at biofuel pump stations throughout the U.S. and outputs valuable data that customers can use to measure nation-wide biofuel production status. This project resulted in reducing the number of man-hours required to monitor biofuel production by over 80%.
